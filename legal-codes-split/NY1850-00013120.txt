The term “justice,” as used in this chapter, includes justice of the peace and justices of justices’ courts in cities; the term “town,” includes ward or district, in reference to justices’ courts in cities; and whenever other sections of the code are made applicable to justices’ courts, the term “judge” and “court” therein, are to be deemed to include justice and justices’ courts; and the term “sheriff,” to include constable.
