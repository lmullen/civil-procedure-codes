The evidence with the order endorsed or attached, must be filed and recorded at length in the office of the clerk of the county, where the land was sold, and thereupon operates as a conveyance of the title of both the mortgagor and mortgagee to the purchaser, and, as a foreclosure of the equity of redemption of every person, upon whom the notice was served as determined in the order of confirmation, whose interest is derived from the mortgagor subsequent to the mortgage, or from a lien attached since that time.
