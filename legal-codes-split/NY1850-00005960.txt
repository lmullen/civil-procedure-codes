For the purpose of admission, he must apply to the supreme court in the district where he resides, on the first day of a general term, and must shew, 1. That he is a citizen of the United States, of the age of twenty-one years; which proof may be made by his own affidavit: and, 2. That he is a person of good moral character; which may be proved by certificate, or other evidence satisfactory to the court.
