, In case of the death of a judgment debtor after judgment, the heirs, devisees, legatees or personal representatives of the judgment debtor, or the tenants of real property, owned by him and affected by the judgment, may be summoned to show cause why the judgment should not be enforced, against the estate of the judgment debtor in their hands respectively. The proceedings thereon are subject to the provisions of the chapter upon actions by or against executors, administrators, legatees, heirs and devisees.
