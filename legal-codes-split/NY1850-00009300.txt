On a judgment for the plaintifff upon an issue of law, the plaintifff may proceed in the manner prescribed by the first two subdivisions of section 755, upon the failure of the defendant to answer, where the summons was personally served. If judgment be for the defendant, upon an issue of law, and the taking of an account or the proof of any fact be necessary to enable the court to complete the judgment, a reference may be ordered, as in that section provided.
