If the property be not removed, and the damage so certified, with the fees of the appraisers and the expense of the notice, be not paid at the time appointed for the sale, the distrainer must apply to the sheriff of the county or a constable of the town, to sell the property, and for that purpose must deliver to the officer the original certificate of the appraisers, and an affidavit, showing his compliance with the provisions of this chapter.
