The court must, in the order for sale, direct the terms of credit which may be allowed, for the purchase money, of any portion of the premises of which it may direct a sale on credit, and for that portion of which the purchase money is required, by the provisions hereinafter contained, to be invested for the benefit of unknown owners, infants, parties out of the state, or tenants in dower, or by the curtesy, or for life or years, when the estate of such tenants is ordered to be sold.
