The proportion of the proceeds of the sale to be invested, must be ascertained and determined in the several cases as follows: 1. If an estate in dower be included in the order of sale, its proportion must be one-third of the proceeds of the sale of the property, or of the sale of the individual share in such property, upon which the claim of dower existed: 2. If an estate by the curtesy, or other estate for life or years, be included in the order of sale, its proportion must be the whole proceeds of the sale of the property, or of the sale of the undivided share thereof, in which such estate may be: And in all cases, the proportion of the expenses of proceedings must be deducted, from the proceeds of the sale.
