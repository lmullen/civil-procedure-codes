If a commission to take such testimony have been issued from the court or judge, before whom such action or proceeding is pending, on producing the commission, to a judge of the supreme court, or a county judge, with an affidavit, satisfactory to him, of the materiality of the testimony, he may issue a subpoena to the witness, requiring him to appear and testify, before the commissioner named in the commission, at a specified time and place.
