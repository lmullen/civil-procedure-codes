The summons may be served by the sheriff of the county where the defendant is found, or by any other person, not a party to the action, the service must be made, and the summons returned, with proof of the service, to the person whose name is subscribed thereto, with all reasonable diligence. The person subscribing the summons, may, at his option, by an endorsement on the summons, fix a time for the service thereof, and the service must then be made accordingly.
