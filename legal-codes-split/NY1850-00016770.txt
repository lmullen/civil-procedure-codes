Assessments, whether for benefit or damage, authorized by any statute of this state, must, in the first instance, be made by three commissioners appointed by the county court of the county, in the following cases. 1. When specific property, real or personal, is taken from the owner and appropriated to public purposes: 2. When the owner of property is required to use, or dispose of, it in a particular manner, for the public benefit; and 3. When a contribution of money is required, towards the expense of local improvement.
