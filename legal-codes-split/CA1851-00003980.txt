I.

PROCEEDINGS AGAINST JOINT DEBTORS.

7 § 368. When a judgmentis recovered against one or more of several
, persons, jointly indebted upon an obligation, by proceeding, as pro’ vided in section 32, those who were not originally served with the
summons, and did not appear to the action, may be summoned to show
cause why they should not be bound by the judgment, in the same
manner as though they had been originally served with the summons.
