for wages against the same, the attachments being issued for other demands
than such wages, shall file an affidavit of his claim, setting forth the amount
and the particular service rendered, with the clerk of the court; and thereafter no attachment shall be discharged upon tiling an undertaking, unless
the amount of such claim, or the amount determined as provided in the
