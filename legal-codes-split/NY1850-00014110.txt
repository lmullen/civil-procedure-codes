A notice or other paper is valid and effectual though the title of the action in which it is made, be omitted, or be defective either in respect to the court or parties, if it intelligibly refer to such action or proceeding, and, in furtherance of justice, upon proper terms, any other defect or error in any notice, or other paper or proceeding, may be amended by the court, and any mischance relieved within one year thereafter.
