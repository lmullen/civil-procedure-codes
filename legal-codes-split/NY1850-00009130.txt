Except as provided in sections 777 and 786, or in case of some accident or calamity, requiring their discharge, the jury cannot be discharged after the cause is submitted to them, until they have agreed upon their verdict, and rendered it in open court, unless by the consent of both parties entered upon the minutes, or unless at the expiration of such time as the court deem proper, it satisfactorily appear, that there is no probability of an agreement.
