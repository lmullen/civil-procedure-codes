The persons entitled to such estate in dower, tenancy by the curtesy, or tenancy for life or years, whose estate shall have been sold, are entitled to receive such sum in gross, as may be deemed upon principles of law applicable to annuities, a reasonable satisfaction for such estate, and which the person so entitled may consent to accept instead thereof; by an instrument under seal, duly acknowledged or proved, in in same manner as deeds for the purpose of record.
