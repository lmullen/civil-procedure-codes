The defendant may demur to the complaint, within twenty days after the service of a copy thereof; when it appears upon the face thereof, either, 1. That the court has no jurisdiction of the person of the defendant, or the subject of the action: or, 2. That the plaintifff has not legal capacity to sue: or, 3. That there is another action pending between the same parties, for the same cause: or, 4. That there is a defect of parties, plaintifff or defendant: or, 5. That several causes of action have been improperly united: or, 6. That the complaint does not state facts sufficient to constitute a cause of action.
