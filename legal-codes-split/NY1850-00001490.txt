This court must be held, except as provided in the next section, by the recorder or city judge of the city of New-York, or by one of the judges of the court of common pleas of that city, as the presiding judge, together with two of the aldermen of the city, designated, as the common council may by ordinance prescribe. But it is the especial duty of the recorder or city judge to hold the court and preside therein.
