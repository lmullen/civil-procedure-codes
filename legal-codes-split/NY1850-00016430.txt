When the distress is of beasts, and the damage and fees are not paid, the distrainer, within twenty-four hours after the appraisement must cause the beasts distrained to be put into the nearest pound in the city or town, if there be one, there to remain till the payment of the damages and fees, or the sale of the boasts as prescribed in section 1462, or until the beats are legally reclaimed. He must also deliver the certificate of the fence viewers to the pound master.
