This court has jurisdiction, 1. To inquire, by the intervention of a grand jury, of all public offenses committed or triable in the city and county of New-York: 2. To try and determine any indictment found therein, or sent thereto by the court of oyer and terminer of the city and county of New-York, for a public offense not punishable with death: 3. To remove police justices and justices of the justices courts of the city of New-York, and their clerks, after due notice and an opportunity of being heard in their defense, for causes to be stated in the order of removal: 4. In cases arising in the city and county of New-York, such as are enumerated in subdivisions 4 to 11, both inclusive of section 125.
