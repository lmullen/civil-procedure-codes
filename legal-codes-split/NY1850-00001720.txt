The jurisdiction conferred by the last section does not extend, however, to a civil action, 1. In which the title to real property shall come in question: 2. Nor for an assault, battery, false imprisonment, libel, slander, malicious prosecution, criminal conversation or seduction, or upon a promise to marry: 3. Nor involving a matter of account, when the sum total of the accounts of both parties, proved to the satisfaction of the court, exceeds four hundred dollars: 4. Nor of an action against an executor or administrator, as such.
