A copy of the complaint and annexed paper must be served by the sheriff, on the defendant, or on any of the several defend ants jointly indebted, by delivering as in the service of a summons in a civil action, with a notice in writing, from the plaintifff or his attorney, that the complaint will be presented, for judgment thereon, to a judge of the court, or, if the complaint be entitled in the supreme court, to a county judge, at a specified time and place; the time to be not less than forty-eight hours after the service, and the place to be within the county, where the service is made, and where the defendant has his place of residence or business.
