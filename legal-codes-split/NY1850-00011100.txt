The conveyances shall also be a bar against all persons having specific or general liens or encumbrances by judgment, on any undivided share or interest in the premises sold, who shall have been served with notice as prescribed by section 939; and also against all persons having specific liens on any undivided share or interest therein, who shall have been made parties to the action. But no creditor having such specific lien can be affected by the sale or conveyance unless he has been made party.
