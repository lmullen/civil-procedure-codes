VII. DRAWING AND SUMMONING THE TRIAL JURY FOR THE MARINE COURT OF THE CITY OF NEW-YORK.

Trial jury for marine court.
To be drawn for every alternate Monday.
Drawing, how conducted.
Jury list to be delivered to a constable or policeman.
Jurors to be summoned three days before court.
Fine may be imposed for not attending.
