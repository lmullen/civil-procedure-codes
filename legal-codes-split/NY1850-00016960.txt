The provisions of the statutes, regulating the proceedings of commissioners, or other persons, appointed to estimate or assess the expense of local improvements, in the several cities and villages of this state, or relating to such assessments, or to the sale or lease of real property, for the failure to pay assessments, so far as the same are consistent with the provisions of this chapter, remain in force; the term “commissioners,” being taken to include all persons appointed to estimate or assess such damages.
