The actions authorized by sections 731 and 736, to be brought by the sheriff, may be prosecuted by the plaintifff, or under his direction, upon the delivery by him to the sheriff of an undertaking executed by two sufficient sureties, to the effect, that the plaintifff will indemnify the sheriff from all damages, costs and charges on account thereof; not exceeding two hundred and fifty dollars in any one action. Such sureties shall in all cases, when required by the sheriff, justify, by making an affidavit that each is a householder, and worth double the amount of the penalty: of the bond, over and above all debts and liabilities.
