The surrogate must also issue a citation to the insane person, to appear at the time and place specified, and show cause, if he have any, against the application. Such citation, with a copy of the application, must be served by the sheriff, at least six days before the time of appearance, on the insane person or drunkard and on some member of the family of such person, or of the family in which he resides, of suitable age and discretion, other than the applicant.
