The defendant may give bail, by causing a written undertaking
to be executed by two or more sufiicient sureties, stating their places of
residence and occupations, to the effect that they are bound in the amount
mentioned in the order of arrest; that the defendant shall at all times render himself amenable to the process of the court during the pendency of
the action, and to such as may be issued to enforce the judgment therein ;
or that they will pay to the plaintifff the amount of any judgment which
may be recovered in the action.
