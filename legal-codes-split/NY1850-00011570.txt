In cases where the next of kin, legatees, heirs, and devisees are liable for the debts of their ancestors: as herein provided, they must give preference in the payment of the same, and are liable thereforee, in the following order: 1. Debts entitled to a preference under the laws of the United States: 2. Judgments against the ancestor or testator according to the priority thereof respectively: 3. Recognizances; bonds, sealed instruments, notes, bills, and unliquidated demands and accounts.
