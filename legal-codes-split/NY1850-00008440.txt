In an action for the recovery of money against a corporation, created by or under the laws of any other state government or country, or against a defendant who is not a resident of this state, or against a defendant who has absconded or concealed himself as hereinafter mentioned, the plaintifff, at the time of issuing the summons, or at any time afterwards, may have the property of such defendant attached, in the manner hereinafter prescribed, as a security for the satisfaction of such judgment as the plaintifff may recover.
