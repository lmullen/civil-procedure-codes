A copy of the application must be served by delivery in the manner required for the service of a summons to commence an action against an infant, with a notice stating that the same will be presented to the court at a specified time and place, and that application to appoint a special guardian will then be made. The service must be at least ten days before the time of application, and two days in addition for every fifty miles distance from the residence of the infant to the place specified.
