When the defendant fails to appear and answer, judgment must be given for the plaintifff, as follows: 1. Where a copy of the complaint and account, or instrument, has been served personally with the summons, as prescribed in section 1069, judgment must be given without further evidence for the sum specified in the summons: 2. In other cases the justice must hear the evidence of the plaintifff, and render judgment for such sum only as shall appear by the evidence to be just; but in no case to exceed the amount specified in the summons.
