Upon proof of personal service upon the person, who is, or is expected to be the adverse party, of the order, copy of the affidavit, and of a notice that the examination will be taken before a judge of the supreme court, or county judge of the county wherein the witness resides or may be at a specified time and place, such judge may take the deposition of the witness conditionally, and the examination may, if necessary, be adjourned from time to time.
