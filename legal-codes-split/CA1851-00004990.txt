This writ may be granted on application by any Court of this
State, except a Justice’s, or Recorder’s, or Mayor’s Court; the writ shall
be granted in all cases when an inferior tribunal, board, or officer, exercising judicial functions, has exceeded the jurisdiction of such tribunal,
board, or odicer; and there is no appeal, nor, in the judgment of the
Court, any plain, speedy, and adequate remedy.
