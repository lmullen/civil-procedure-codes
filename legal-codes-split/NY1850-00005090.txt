A person who sells or brings into jail intoxicating liquors, contrary to the last section, and a sheriff or other officer employed about a jail, who knowingly suffers them to be sold or used contrary thereto, is guilty of a misdemeanor, and on conviction, is punishable by imprisonment not exceeding one year, or by a fine not exceeding two hundred and fifty dollars, or both; and a sheriff so convicted, shall forfeit his office.
