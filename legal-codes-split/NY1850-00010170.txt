The clerk may receiver, On every trial, from the party bringing it on, one dollar: On entering judgment, fifty cents except in courts where the clerks receive salaries, and in such courts one dollar: On docketing a judgment rendered by a justice in his county, and filing a transcript thereof, twenty-five cents, including execution when issued. He can receive no other fee, for any services whatever in a civil action, except for copies of papers, at the rate of five cents for every hundred words.
