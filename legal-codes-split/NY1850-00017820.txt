When there is not personal property, sufficient with the income of the real property, for the payment of the debts, or for the proper maintenance of the insane person or drunkard person and his family, the surrogate may upon a proper accounting by the committee, of the personal, and of the income of the real, property, order the real property or such part thereof as he may deem necessary, to be sold or mortgaged to procure the necessary funds thereforee.
